About CAWF

Cine Artist & Workers Welfare Federation (CAWF) is a welfare organization that supports artists and workers in the film, television, and entertainment industry. We aim to protect their rights, provide opportunities, and offer support in times of need.

Our mission is to uplift the lives of those working behind and in front of the camera by offering guidance, training, legal help, medical aid, and financial support. Whether you're an actor, technician, writer, or worker, we stand by your side for a better and secure future.

Our Story

In the glittering world of cinema, thousands of dreams shine every day — not just on-screen, but behind the scenes too. From the lightman who sets the mood, to the spot boy running tirelessly, to the junior artist giving life to the crowd — every frame of entertainment carries the effort of unsung heroes.
Cine Artist & Workers Welfare Federation (CAWF) was born from a simple yet powerful idea: "Every hand that builds cinema deserves dignity, respect, and security."

Founded by a group of passionate film professionals and social leaders, CAWF is more than just a federation — it's a movement to uplift, protect, and empower every individual connected to the film and television industry. Whether you're a makeup artist, actor, technician, choreographer, or even part of the backstage crew — CAWF stands with you.
