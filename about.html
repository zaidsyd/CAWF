about CAWF

Building a Stronger Industry Together

Cine Artist & Worker Welfare Federation (CAWF) is a dedicated organization committed to the welfare and empowerment of professionals in the Indian film and television industry. We provide support, advocacy, and skill development opportunities to cine artists and workers, ensuring their rights and well-being are protected.

  • Professional Mentors & Coaches

our approach

Inspiring Creative Excellence Worldwide

At CAWF, we are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for all cine artists and workers. We prioritize their welfare by providing timely assistance, advocating for their rights, and creating opportunities for skill development.

Our mission is to uplift the creative workforce and entertainment industry by empowering artists and workers through education, welfare initiatives, and social security programs, building a stronger, more inclusive creative community.

  • Skill Development & Training Programs
  • Healthcare & Insurance Support
  • Social Security & Welfare Schemes
  • Artist Empowerment & Cultural Growth

Our vision is to build a future where every artist and worker in the creative industry thrives with dignity, opportunity, and protection — creating a sustainable ecosystem that values talent, promotes equality, and nurtures cultural excellence.

  • Inclusive Growth for All Creatives
  • Equal Opportunities Across the Industry
  • Long-Term Social and Economic Security
  • A Thriving, Respected Artistic Community

Our core values guide every initiative we take. We are committed to building a creative industry where integrity, respect, and innovation empower artists and workers to reach their fullest potential with pride and purpose.

  • Integrity and Transparency

our history

Foundation of Excellence inEntertainment Industry

Built on a legacy of trust and dedication, we have created a solid foundation in the film and television sector, consistently supporting artists and workers with welfare, advocacy, and skill development programs that uplift the community and shape industry standards.

Vision Established

Welcome to CAWF, a forward-thinking federation built on the vision of a fair, empowered, and inclusive entertainment industry. In 2020, we laid the groundwork for an organization dedicated to supporting the lives and careers of cine artists and workers

  • Welfare & Support Systems
  • Skill Development Initiatives
  • Ethical & Fair Practices
  • Promoting transparency

Official Launch

In 2021, CAWF was officially registered as a non-profit welfare federation, marking a major milestone in our journey. This formal recognition empowered us to operate at a larger scale, reach more industry professionals, and strengthen our voice as a representative body.

  • Legal Identity & Governance
  • Membership Enrollment Begins
  • Partnership Building
  • Foundation for Welfare Programs

First Welfare Initiatives

With the foundation firmly in place, 2022 marked the beginning of an extensive outreach campaign. CAWF actively connected with cine artists, technicians, and workers across regions to raise awareness about their rights, benefits, and opportunities through the federation.

  • On-Ground Awareness Drives
  • Digital Enrollment Platform Launched
  • Community Building Events
  • Feedback & Listening Forums

Expanded Membership

As our welfare initiatives gained momentum, 2023 also became a landmark year for membership growth. CAWF expanded its reach to include a broader spectrum of industry professionals — from junior artists and backstage crew to emerging talent in regional cinema.

  • Pan-India Representation
  • Inclusive Enrollment Policies
  • Member Benefits Introduced
  • Growing Community, Stronger Voice

Industry Collaborations

In 2024, CAWF expanded its impact through key partnerships with production houses, training institutes, and government bodies, strengthening welfare programs and creating more opportunities and protections for cine artists and workers.

our key feature

Strengthening the Creative Workforce

Our commitment lies in empowering every artist and industry worker through real support, fair opportunities, and consistent advocacy. We work to uplift the film, fashion, and creative communities by providing essential services, education, and a voice where it's needed most.

Professional Training & Career Development

We provide structured training programs in modeling, acting, photography, videography, and cinematography — equipping aspiring and working professionals with the skills needed to thrive in today’s competitive creative landscape.

Protection of Rights & Fair Support

CAWF actively supports artists and workers facing workplace injustice or exploitation. Through our welfare services, we ensure timely help, legal guidance, and fair representation for every member in need.

Empowerment Through Opportunities & Recognition

We continuously create opportunities for talent to shine — from fashion events and short films to talent hunts and award shows. By recognizing achievements and enabling industry access, CAWF helps artists and workers grow with dignity and direction.
